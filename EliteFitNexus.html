  1. Membership and Access:

    Only members with valid memberships or day passes are allowed entry.
    Members must scan their membership card or provide identification upon entry.

  2. Attire:

    Proper athletic attire, including athletic shoes, must be worn at all times.
    Shirts and shoes are usually required in workout areas.

  3. Equipment Use:

    Follow proper etiquette when using equipment (e.g., wiping down machines after use).
    Allow others to "work in" or share equipment during busy times.

  4. Safety:

    Use equipment as intended and follow safety guidelines.
    Do not drop weights on the floor.

  5. Hygiene:

    Towels are often required on benches and machines to maintain cleanliness.
    Wipe down equipment after use.

  6. Noise and Disturbance:

    Keep noise to a minimum, including dropping weights quietly.
    Use headphones for personal audio devices.

  7. Personal Belongings:

    Use lockers to store personal belongings.
    Do not leave personal items unattended.

  8. Respect for Others:

    Be mindful of others' personal space.
    Avoid loud or disruptive behavior.

  9. Group Classes:

    Arrive on time for scheduled classes.
    Follow the instructor's guidelines.

  10. Children and Guests:

    Children may have restricted access or specific hours.
    Guests may need to sign in and adhere to gym policies.
